Getting Started

Pelfmont Markets is a fully non-custodial prediction market built on Solana. Everything happens on-chain — no accounts, no databases, no middlemen.

Tap Connect in the top-right corner. Phantom and Solflare wallets are supported on desktop. On mobile, the app uses the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter. If you have a .sol domain, it will display in the wallet button and throughout the app.

Exploring Markets

The Explore tab is your home screen. Each market card shows outcome probability bars, volume, USD value, position count, time remaining, and maker (with SNS name when available). If you have a position in a market, an estimate badge shows your projected payout with USD value right on the card.

Status filter — Use the dropdown to filter by Open, Closed (past deadline, awaiting resolution), Resolved, Finalized, Voided, or All. Default is Open.

My Markets — Toggle the button to show only markets where you are the authority or maker.

Street Bets — Toggle the button to show only markets with a total runtime of 30 minutes or less. These fast-fire markets are tagged with an orange "Street Bet" badge on cards and detail pages.

Sort — Sort by Closing Soon (default), Deadline, Newest/Oldest, Value High/Low, or Most/Least Positions. Additional filters for maker, category, and token denomination.

Hot Markets chart — Toggle the bar chart to see the top 10 open markets by USD volume. Closed and voided markets are excluded. Click any bar to jump to that market.

Market Detail

Tap any market card to open its detail page. You'll see the full description, outcome distribution charts with USD values, pool breakdown, authority and maker addresses (with SNS names), fee structure, and denomination.

If you hold a position, estimate badges appear showing your projected payout with USD equivalent (or win/loss once finalized).

Use the Share button to send the market link to others via your device's native share sheet, or it will copy the link to your clipboard.

Choosing a Position

On the market detail page (open markets only), select the outcome you want to take a position on, enter your amount, and review the estimated payout with USD value. The estimate updates live based on current pool sizes and the total fee.

For SOL markets, the minimum position is 0.01 SOL. Token markets have no minimum — any amount works depending on the token.

Tap Confirm Position and approve the transaction in your wallet. Your funds go directly into the on-chain vault — not to any company or individual.

Markets can be denominated in native SOL, SPL tokens, or Token-2022 tokens. The denomination is shown on the market card and detail page.

Markets that are past their deadline show as Awaiting Resolution and no longer accept new positions.

Market Lifecycle

Open — Accepting positions. Anyone can take a position on any outcome.

Awaiting Resolution — The deadline has passed but the authority hasn't resolved yet. No new positions accepted. Displayed as "Closed" in the status filter.

Resolved — The authority declared a winning outcome. A 24-hour dispute window begins. No new positions.

Finalized — The dispute window has passed and the market has been finalized. Winners can claim payouts. Finalization is permissionless — anyone can trigger it once the window expires.

Voided — The authority cancelled the market. All participants can claim full refunds regardless of outcome.

Dispute Window

After a market is resolved, a 24-hour dispute window begins before the market can be finalized. This gives participants time to review the outcome and gives the authority a chance to correct mistakes.

Change Resolution — During the dispute window, the market authority can change the winning outcome to a different one. This restarts the full 24-hour window so everyone has time to review the updated result.

Void Market — The authority can also void the market during the dispute window, which cancels it entirely and allows all participants to claim full refunds.

Once the 24-hour window passes without any changes, anyone can finalize the market by clicking the Finalize Market button. No special permissions are needed to finalize.

Positions — Filtering & Sorting

The Positions tab shows all your positions across every market. Use the filters to zero in on what you need:

Category — Filter by the market's on-chain category.

Status — Show only positions in Open, Closed (past deadline), Resolved, Finalized, or Voided markets.

Result — Filter by Unclaimed, Claimed, Winning, or Losing. Use "Winning + Unclaimed" together to quickly find everything you need to claim.

Sort — Order by deadline, newest/oldest, position size, estimated payout, or group by market status.

Each position card shows the outcome, amount, deposit date, deadline, and maker. USD values are shown on estimate badges when price data is available.

If a market is finalized and you hold the winning outcome, a Claim Winnings button appears. For voided markets, you'll see Claim Refund.

Making a Market

Go to the Make tab. Fill in a title, optional category, description, and define 2–10 outcomes. Set a resolution deadline.

Maker Fee — Optionally set an additional fee (0–500 bps / up to 5%) that you earn on top of the protocol default. This is paid out of winning claims directly to your wallet.

Denomination — Choose Native SOL, SPL Token, or Token-2022. For token markets, paste the mint address and a live preview with the token icon, name, and symbol will appear from on-chain metadata.

The category is stored on-chain inside the description and displayed as a badge on market cards. As the maker, you are the market authority — responsible for resolving with the correct outcome or voiding if needed.

Watchlist

Tap the ☆ star on any market card to add it to your watchlist. A picker appears with your existing categories and a field to create a new one inline — just type a name and tap +.

In the Watchlist tab, filter by category tabs, reassign categories on individual cards, and manage your categories with the ⚙ button. Categories are sorted alphabetically.

Trash — A built-in protected category that always appears last. Move unwanted markets to Trash instead of removing them. Use the Clear Trash button to permanently remove all trashed markets.

Export / Import — In the category manager (⚙), use Export to save your entire watchlist as a .json file, and Import to merge a saved watchlist back in. Importing adds new markets and categories without duplicating existing ones.

Your watchlist is stored locally in your browser.

Sharing

There are two ways to share. The share icon in the top-right nav bar shares whatever page you're on. On market detail pages, the dedicated Share button includes the market title for richer context.

On mobile, this opens your device's native share sheet. On desktop, it copies the link to your clipboard.

Every market has a permanent deep link: #/market/<address>. Anyone who opens it lands directly on that market.

Fees

Fees are charged on winning claims only — never on losing positions, voided refunds, or market creation.

The total fee is split between the protocol treasury (the default fee set by the admin) and the market maker (any additional fee they set). The fee breakdown is visible on each market's detail page.

Protocol Pause

The protocol admin can pause the system, which prevents new markets from being created and new positions from being placed. Claims and refunds always remain available even when paused — your funds are never locked.

Tips

• Outcome bars show implied probabilities based on pool sizes — they update live as positions come in.

• Payout estimates with USD values appear on market cards, position cards, and the bet input when you have an active position.

• Data refreshes automatically every 20 seconds on the Explore page.

• Priority fee level and compute tolerance are configurable via environment variables (PRIORITY and TOLERANCE) at build time.

• This app has zero backend infrastructure. If the website goes down, your funds are always safe on-chain and recoverable with any Solana program explorer.

Powered by Precog Markets

Pelfmont Markets is built on top of the Precog Markets protocol — a trustless pari-mutuel prediction market program deployed on the Solana blockchain. The protocol handles all market creation, position management, resolution, dispute windows, finalization, and payouts entirely on-chain with no intermediaries.

The protocol supports native SOL, SPL Token, and Token-2022 denominations (including mints with transfer fee extensions), on-chain M-of-N multisig governance for market authorities, and a permissionless finalization model where anyone can crank finalization once the dispute window passes.

The open-source JavaScript SDK that powers this app is available as an npm package for developers who want to build their own interfaces or integrations on the same protocol.
