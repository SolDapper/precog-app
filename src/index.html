How to Use Pelfmont Markets

Getting Started

Pelfmont Markets is a fully non-custodial prediction market built on Solana. Everything happens on-chain — no accounts, no databases, no middlemen.

Tap Connect in the top-right corner. Phantom and Solflare wallets are supported on desktop. On mobile, the app uses the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter. If you have a .sol domain, it will display in the wallet button and throughout the app.

Exploring Markets

The Explore tab is your home screen. Each market card shows outcome probability bars, volume, position count, and time remaining. If you have a position in a market, an estimate badge shows your projected payout right on the card.

Status tabs — Filter by Open, Resolved, Finalized, or All. The "My Markets" tab shows only markets you created.

Sort & filter — Sort by Closing Soon, Volume, or Most Positions. Filter by creator (with SNS name display), category, or token denomination.

Hot Markets chart — Toggle the bar chart to see the top 10 markets by volume. Voided markets are excluded. Click any bar to jump to that market.

Market Detail

Tap any market card to open its detail page. You'll see the full description, outcome distribution charts, pool breakdown, authority and creator addresses, fee structure, and denomination.

If you hold a position, estimate badges appear next to the watchlist button showing your projected payout (or win/loss once finalized).

Use the Share button to send the market link to others via your device's native share sheet, or it will copy the link to your clipboard.

Choosing a Position

On the market detail page, select the outcome you want to take a position on, enter your amount, and review the estimated payout. The estimate updates live based on current pool sizes and the total fee.

Tap Confirm Position and approve the transaction in your wallet. Your funds go directly into the on-chain vault — not to any company or individual.

Markets can be denominated in native SOL, SPL tokens, or Token-2022 tokens. The denomination is shown on the market card and detail page.

Market Lifecycle

Open — Accepting positions. Anyone can take a position on any outcome.

Resolved — The authority declared a winning outcome. A 24-hour dispute window begins. No new positions.

Finalized — The dispute window has passed. Winners can claim payouts.

Voided — The authority cancelled the market. All participants can claim full refunds.

Positions — Filtering & Sorting

The Positions tab shows all your positions across every market. Use the filters to zero in on what you need:

Category — Filter by the market's on-chain category.

Status — Show only positions in Open, Resolved, Finalized, or Voided markets.

Result — Filter by Unclaimed, Claimed, Winning, or Losing. Use "Winning + Unclaimed" together to quickly find everything you need to claim.

Sort — Order by deadline, position size, estimated payout, or group by market status.

If a market is finalized and you hold the winning outcome, a Claim Winnings button appears. For voided markets, you'll see Claim Refund.

Creating a Market

Go to the Create tab. Fill in a title, optional category, description, and define 2–10 outcomes. Set a resolution deadline.

Creator Fee — Optionally set an additional fee (0–500 bps / up to 5%) that you earn on top of the protocol default. This is paid out of winning claims directly to your wallet.

Denomination — Choose Native SOL, SPL Token, or Token-2022. For token markets, paste the mint address and a live preview with the token icon, name, and symbol will appear from on-chain metadata.

The category is stored on-chain inside the description and displayed as a badge on market cards. As the creator, you are the market authority — responsible for resolving with the correct outcome or voiding if needed.

Watchlist

Tap the ☆ star on any market card to add it to your watchlist with a personal category. Your watchlist is stored locally in your browser.

In the Watchlist tab, filter by category, reassign categories, and manage your custom categories with the ⚙ button.

Sharing

There are two ways to share. The share icon in the top-right nav bar shares whatever page you're on. On market detail pages, the dedicated Share button includes the market title for richer context.

On mobile, this opens your device's native share sheet. On desktop, it copies the link to your clipboard.

Every market has a permanent deep link: #/market/<address>. Anyone who opens it lands directly on that market.

Fees

Fees are charged on winning claims only — never on losing positions, voided refunds, or market creation.

The total fee is split between the protocol treasury (the default fee set by the admin) and the market creator (any additional fee they set). The fee breakdown is visible on each market's detail page.

Tips

• Outcome bars show implied probabilities based on pool sizes — they update live as positions come in.

• Payout estimates appear on both market cards and position cards when you have an active position.

• Data refreshes automatically every 20 seconds on the Explore page.

• This app has zero backend infrastructure. If the website goes down, your funds are always safe on-chain and recoverable with any Solana program explorer.
