How to Use Pelfmont Markets

Getting Started

Pelfmont Markets is a fully non-custodial prediction market built on Solana. Everything happens on-chain — no accounts, no databases, no middlemen. Connect your Solana wallet to start.

Tap Connect in the top-right corner. Phantom, Solflare, and Jupiter wallets are supported on desktop. On mobile, the app uses the Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter.

Exploring Markets

The Explore tab is your home screen. It lists all on-chain markets with outcome probability bars, volume, position count, and time remaining.

Filters: Use the status tabs (Open, Resolved, Finalized, All) to narrow by market state. The "My Markets" filter shows only markets you created.

Sort: Choose between Closing Soon (default), Volume, or Most Positions.

Creator filter: Use the Creator dropdown to see markets from a specific creator. Creators with .sol names will display their SNS identity.

Hot Markets: Toggle the chart to see the top 10 markets by volume at a glance.

Placing a Bet

Tap any market card to open its detail page. Select the outcome you want to bet on, enter your amount in SOL, and review the estimated payout. The payout estimate updates live based on current pool sizes and the protocol fee.

Tap Place Bet and approve the transaction in your wallet. Your SOL goes directly into the on-chain vault — not to any company or individual.

Market Lifecycle

Open — Accepting bets. Anyone can place a bet on any outcome.

Resolved — The market authority has declared a winning outcome. A 24-hour dispute window begins. No new bets accepted.

Finalized — The dispute window has passed. Winners can now claim their payouts.

Voided — The authority cancelled the market. All bettors can claim full refunds.

Claiming Winnings & Refunds

Go to the Positions tab to see all your bets. If a market has been finalized and you bet on the winning outcome, a Claim Winnings button will appear. For voided markets, you'll see Claim Refund.

Payouts are proportional: your share of the total pool equals your share of the winning outcome's pool, minus the protocol and creator fees.

Creating a Market

Go to the Create tab. Fill in a title, optional category, description, and define 2–10 outcomes. Set a resolution deadline and an optional creator fee (0–500 basis points) — this is the additional fee you earn on top of the protocol default.

The category you set is stored on-chain inside the description field and displayed as a badge on market cards.

As the market creator (authority), you are responsible for resolving the market with the correct outcome before or after the deadline. You can also void the market if needed.

Watchlist

Tap the ☆ star on any market card to add it to your watchlist. A popup lets you choose a category for your personal organization. Your watchlist is stored locally in your browser.

In the Watchlist tab you can filter by category, reassign categories, and manage your custom categories using the ⚙ button.

Deep Links

Every market has a shareable URL. When viewing a market, the browser URL updates to #/market/<address>. Copy and share this link — anyone who opens it will land directly on that market's detail page.

Fees & Protocol

Fees are charged on winning claims only. The protocol takes its default fee (set by the admin) which goes to the protocol treasury. Market creators can add an additional creator fee (up to 5%) which goes directly to the creator's wallet. There are no fees on losing bets, voided refunds, or market creation.

Tips

• The outcome bars show implied probabilities based on pool sizes — they update as bets come in.

• Your .sol domain name will display in the wallet button and on market authority fields if you have one set.

• All data refreshes automatically every 20 seconds on the Explore page.

• This app has zero backend infrastructure. If the website goes down, your funds are always safe on-chain and recoverable with any Solana explorer.
